Installation
============

1) Place this directory in your modules directory.

2) Enable the module at admin/build/modules.

3) Configure, edit, add, enable, disable, or delete flexifilters at admin/build/flexifilters.

4) Add your custom filters to input formats at admin/settings/filters.
